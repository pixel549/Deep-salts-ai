Triggered Insanity saves for both to establish the psychological pressure of Stillwell Hydro's vacant, humid halls. No NPCs revealed yet; the reception clerk remains hidden for now.

I have presented three distinct directions to split their focus: the reception desk itself (and the guest register), the locked/dark Administration office (which houses the records for the 'personnel_file_discrepancy' trigger), and the lower corridor (leading toward maintenance and the depths). I will watch to see if Maren tries to access Administration immediately under her auditor guise, or if Corwin tries to find a path down to the salt source.

Maren's failed search of the ledger yielded nothing but a slight sanity hit. The Reception Clerk has been revealed; he is executing his programmed loop ('taking the deep cure'). He will not help them or let them see anyone. This creates a hard wall in the lobby, forcing them to either bribe him, bypass him, or find another way into the facility (such as the administration office to search records, which would trigger the personnel file discrepancy).
